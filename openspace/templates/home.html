What We Do

You want a website, and you want that website fast, free, and easy. Welcome to Open Space, you're in the right place. We help you build a great looking site from a variety of community built templates, for free. Yes, you read that correctly, totally free. Why? Because we can. We hope you love Open Space, and don't forget to share it with all your friends so that they can build awesome sites with us too. Better yet, if you are a website designer, submit a template!

Why You Care

Websites can be hard to build nowadays. With tens, hundreds, even thousands of languages available for the web, it can be confusing to new developers. HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, PHP, Node.js, Socket.io, SQL, and many more are just a few technologies and languages that are used often on the web, but not everyone has time to learn them all. So we did for you, and made it as simple as entering information on a page in order to create a beautiful website. Have fun.
